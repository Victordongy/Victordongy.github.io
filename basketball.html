We could see that James Harden's 3 point shooting is heavily influenced by the game period, when James plays more than 30 minutes, every one more minutes he plays, his three point shooting is decreasing at rate of 0.03%, which is a large influence for a top player worldwide.

In summary, I really doubt the theory that 33.3% 3 point could beat 50% 2 point in the basketball field, as the shooting percentage is biasedly skewed along with the body fatigue, especially for some players.
